This project showcases a **production-ready Android live streaming encoder** designed for professional broadcasting workflows. The application transforms an Android smartphone into a powerful hardware encoder capable of receiving video from **professional HDMI cameras** through a USB Video Capture device and transmitting low-latency streams using **SRT** and **RTMP** protocols.

Unlike conventional mobile streaming applications, this solution is specifically designed for **broadcast production**, allowing professional cameras to replace expensive dedicated hardware encoders while maintaining excellent video quality, low latency, and reliable transmission.

The application has been **successfully deployed in real-world television production**, where it is integrated into professional live broadcasting workflows.
